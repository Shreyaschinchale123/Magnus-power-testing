About Us--

Welcome to Magnus - Powering
Success Since 1988

Magnus Power is a Turnkey Power Plant builder in Asia for HFO-Diesel-Gas Generators. We design, install and commission Power Plants specifically to the customer's needs. Our complete solutions will help your business achieve a stable energy supply along with substantial cost savings. This is supported by our team of qualified and experienced Engineers, Supervisors, Technicians and other Support Staff to meet all project requirements.

Our complete solutions will help your business achieve a stable energy supply along with substantial cost savings. This is supported by our team of qualified and experienced Engineers, Supervisors, Technicians and other Support Staff to meet all project requirements.

In the last three decades, we have successfully installed 200 captive/IPP Power Plants with cumulative capacity of 2000MW. We have installed Power plants for various industries such as Textiles, Pharmaceuticals, Engineering, Construction, Government sectors, and Electricity boards.

We offer Independent Power Producers (IPP) Projects above 50MW for liquid fuel / Gas based Power Plants mainly focusing on reliability, cost effective and environmental solutions. Magnus Power is associated with the following engine manufacturers, MAN, Wartsila, Rolls Royce, Caterpillar, Zgoda Sulzer etc for Power Plant projects. We have vast experience in Power Plant planning and construction and reliable partner for electricity producers.
