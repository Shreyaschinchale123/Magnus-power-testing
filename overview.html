OVERVIEW

From Spark to Powerhouse: Magnus Power's Story

Magnus Power is a Turnkey Power Plant builder in Asia for HFO-Diesel-Gas Generators.
We design, install and commission Power Plants specifically to the customer's needs.

Our complete solutions will help your business achieve a stable energy supply along with substantial cost savings.
This is supported by our team of qualified and experienced Engineers, Supervisors, Technicians and other Support Staff to meet all project requirements.

In the last three decades, we have successfully installed 200 captive/IPP Power Plants with cumulative capacity of 2000MW.
We have installed Power Plants for various industries such as Textiles, Pharmaceuticals, Engineering, Construction, Government sectors, and Electricity boards.

We offer Independent Power Producers (IPP) Projects above 50MW for liquid fuel / Gas based Power Plants mainly focusing on reliability, cost-effective and environmental solutions.
Magnus Power is associated with engine manufacturers like MAN, Wartsila, Rolls Royce, Caterpillar, Zgoda Sulzer, etc.
We have vast experience in Power Plant planning and construction and are a reliable partner for electricity producers.

Vision & Mission

Empowering the Future of Industries

At Magnus Power, we are committed to transforming the industrial landscape through cutting-edge Diesel Engine Power technology, sustainable practices, and a passion for quality.
