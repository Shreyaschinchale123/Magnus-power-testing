Operation & Maintenance

Operation & Maintenance (O&M) contracts cover all aspects and activities necessary to run the Power Plant in a safe and most economical manner. O&M agreements help minimize risks and maximize profits.

Magnus Power offers OEM expertise and extensive O&M experience, delivering unmatched performance warranties and dependable operations.

With our experienced and highly qualified personnel, we offer:

  • 24/7 Operation
  • Preventive Maintenance
  • Corrective Maintenance
  • Condition Monitoring
  • Remote Monitoring by Expert Center
  • Provision of Spare Parts and Consumables
  • Repair and Refurbishment of Major Components
  • Execution of Scheduled Outages

All services are delivered by our team in accordance with international Quality, Health & Safety, and Environmental standards. We also provide regular updates and improvisations to increase plant efficiency.
