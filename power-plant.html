Power Plants

1 Liquid Fuel Power Plant

Magnus Power is one of the pioneers in designing liquid fuel-based Power Plants. With over 30 years of experience, we deliver optimized power generation systems.

  • Low installation cost
  • Short delivery and installation period
  • Higher efficiency (45%-49%)
  • Less auxiliary consumption
  • More efficient under part loads
  • Suitable for various fuel viscosities

We design the Liquid Fuel Power Plant aux systems to support the entire plant, compatible with various viscose fluids.

2 Gas Power Plant

Magnus Power designs modular Gas-based Power Plants and gas handling systems to ensure smooth installation and long-term reliability.

  • State-of-the-art gas engines
  • Electrical output from 2–15 MW/unit
  • High generation efficiency (up to 50%)
  • CHP efficiency up to 90%
  • Low air pollutant and CO2 emissions
  • Consistent output in wide temperature ranges
  • Exceptional reliability and uptime

Gas engines are ideal for efficient and clean power or CHP generation in areas with piped gas supply.

Efficiency, flexibility, and low environmental impact make Gas Power Plants a superior solution.
