About Manufacturing Facility

On the strength of the expertise gained through the successful execution of diverse EPC projects in association with global leaders in the field, Magnus Power, for the benefit of the customer, has developed a strategy for backward integration.

Accordingly, MPPL has set up a state-of-the-art factory to manufacture various subsystems of power projects with the intention of providing a better value proposition to its customers. The factory is located at Vasanthanarsapura Industrial Estate, an ideal location on the Bengaluru–Pune highway with easy access to airports and seaports.

The factory consists of:

  • Assembly and workshops of approx. 2000 Sq. mts of covered area
  • State-of-the-art general-purpose machining, fabrication and welding equipment along with backup power
  • Metrology and testing systems conforming to national and international standards
  • Fully equipped office with networked environment to support the entire factory operations

The factory has a core team of engineers/technicians trained in manufacturing various subsystems with a focus on quality and performance.

The Quality Management System of the factory is certified to ISO 9001:2015.

The factory is developing as a center for world-class auxiliary systems for liquid and gas-based projects.

Auxiliary Systems Manufactured Include:

  • Ventilation units of various capacities ranging from 30,000 CMH to 1,25,000 CMH
  • Skid-mounted pump modules for various applications with cutting-edge components and instrumentation
  • Air receivers and control air bottles of various capacities
  • Make-up water and expansion tanks of various capacities
