Auxiliary Modules

Magnus Power is committed to deliver unique solutions, meeting international standards in quality and product performance.

Our products are performing at various Power Projects executed by industry leaders like MAN, Wartsila, Rolls-Royce, Caterpillar, etc.

We offer a wide range of modular systems both for operation with heavy fuel oil and for gas or regenerative liquid fuels. Special systems are designed and manufactured for Power Plants with single or multi-engine setups, providing engines with fuel, water, lubricating oil, and air.
